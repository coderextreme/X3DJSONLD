Technical Information

CyberAnatomy 101 was created with Cosmo Worlds 1.0. Medical illustrations & textures were enhanced in Photoshop 3.0. CyberAnatomy 101 contains several VRML 2.0 features, including: spatialized audio and anchors to HTML links.

Animation: CyberAnatomy 101 uses 3D geometric morphing for the opening animation. Alpha transparency animation-fade-in is used for removing and fading out the different layers on the body. Keyframe animation produces the lifelike pumping of the organs. Material animation is used to produce the glowing effect on the Zoom In/Out buttons.
